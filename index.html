About Me

My name is Bahadır Atcı and I'm from Eskişehir, Türkiye. I hold a degree in Visual Arts from Osmangazi University, where I dedicated four years to refining my artistic expertise and developing a strong foundation in creative expression. During my academic journey, I enriched my perspective through the Erasmus+ program, studying at ESAD in Caldas da Rainha, Portugal, and UAD in Cluj-Napoca, Romania.
