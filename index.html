About Me

My name is Bahadır Atcı, and I hail from Eskişehir, Türkiye. I hold four years of formal education in Visual Arts from Osmangazi University. During my academic journey, I had the privilege of studying at ESAD in Caldas da Rainha and UAD in Cluj-Napoca as part of the Erasmus+ program. My artistic focus centers on creating abstract illustrations and celebrating feminine beauty in my work. Since 2015, I have been deeply committed to exploring visual storytelling and continually refining my craft.
